F.A.Q.

Do You Cook Non Carribean Cuisine?
  • Yes. We Can Curate Menus/Meals From Multiple Cuisines.  
  • Let us know what you’re interested in, all of our menus are customizable.
What Is The Maximum Number of Guests You Cater For?

While we’re just a team of two, we can accommodate catering for up to 60 guests. Please inquire should you need catering for up to 100 guests as we can set up a consultation to assess whether we can source additional resources to accommodate your event!!

Do You Travel?

Yes, we can accommodate events in the tri state of New York, New Jersey and Connecticut . Travel costs are added to the cost of the event.

How Far In Advance Do We Need To Book An Event ?
  • We require a minimum of  two weeks from the day of the event to plan and properly execute catering. for an event.  
  • You can book up to 6 months in advance as well.
Do You Have Insurance?

Yes we are fully insured!! We can provide proof of insurance for any needs.

Do You Offer Menu Tastings?

Yes we do!! We offer menu tasting for a small fee. This fee is non-refundable but it is deducted from your catering costs should you choose to book with us.

How Does Booking Work?

Once your catering inquiry has been received, we confirm we can accommodate the request. To move forward we set up an original consultation, and proceed to draw up a contract with the details. A deposit of half of the total catering costs is due at the time of contract signing, and the remaining deposit is due three days prior to the event.
